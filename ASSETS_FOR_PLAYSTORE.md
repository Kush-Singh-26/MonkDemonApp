## Screenshots

Take screenshots from a physical Android device or emulator at these common resolutions:

- **Phone portrait**: 1080 × 1920
- **7″ tablet**: 1200 × 1920
- **10″ tablet**: 1920 × 1200

Minimum 2 per device type. Upload to the Play Console under **App content → Screenshots**.

## Feature Graphic

- 1024 × 500 PNG
- Upload to Play Console under **Store listing → Feature graphic**

## Promotional Video (optional)

- YouTube link or MP4 ≤ 50 MB
- Shown at the top of the Play Store listing

## Full Description (≤ 4000 chars)

> Monk & Demon brings the classic river-crossing logic puzzle to your Android device.
>
> **The Puzzle**: 3 monks and 3 demons must cross a river in one boat that holds at most 2 passengers. The twist — if demons ever outnumber monks on either bank, the monks are in danger.
>
> **Features**:
> - Beautiful hand-crafted aesthetic with medieval parchment feel
> - Three built-in solvers (BFS, DFS, A*) that you can watch explore the search tree in real time
> - Step-by-step auto-play of the optimal solution
> - Undo/redo move history
> - Works fully offline — all fonts bundled
> - Immersive fullscreen on Android
>
> Can you get everyone across safely?
